By: Sofia Rusu, date: 25/01/2025

Dubai is one of the seven emirates that make up the United Arab Emirates (UAE). It's located on the southeast coast of the Persian Gulf and is known for its ultramodern architecture, luxury shopping, and vibrant nightlife.

(3) Popular Tourist Attractions

Dubai is a hotspot for stunning tourist attractions that blend modernity and tradition. Here are some must-visit places

  • The Burj Khalifa: The tallest building in the world with observation decks offering incredible views.

  • Dubai Mall: One of the largest shopping centers globally, featuring an aquarium, ice rink, and an indoor theme park.

  • Palm Jumeirah: A man-made island resembling a palm tree, with luxury hotels and beaches.

  • Dubai Creek: Offering traditional boat (dhow) rides and a peek into the city’s rich history.

  • Dubai Frame: A massive picture frame-like structure offering panoramic views of both old and new Dubai from its glass bridge.

  • Ski Dubai: An indoor ski resort located inside the Mall of the Emirates, featuring real snow, skiing, snowboarding, and penguin encounters.

  • Al Shindagha Historic District: A beautiful place to visit on a relaxing day in Dubai.

(4) Visiting Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi is rich in cultural, historical, and modern attractions. Here’s a brief overview of its key tourist attractions:

  • Louvre Abu Dhabi: An impressive museum showcasing a diverse collection of art, from ancient to contemporary, beneath a striking silver dome.

  • Emirates Palace: A luxurious hotel that epitomizes Arabian grandeur, with beautiful gardens and a private beach.

  • Saadiyat Island: Offers cultural experiences with attractions like the upcoming Guggenheim Abu Dhabi and a serene beach.

  • Heritage Village: A reconstructed traditional oasis village providing a glimpse into the Emirati way of life.

(5) Transport

Dubai has an extensive and varied transportation network, offering multiple ways to get around the city.

  • Dubai Metro: €0.70 to €1.80 depending on the travel zone.
  • Dubai Tram: €0.70 per trip.
  • Public Buses: €2.80 (daytime) to €5.90 (nighttime or airport taxis), with an additional €0.40 per kilometer.
  • Car Rentals: €18.10 to €34 per day for a standard car.
